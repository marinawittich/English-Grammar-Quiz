Hi there! Let's practice English grammar.


are used
is being used
have been used

Correct answer: are used (Present Simple). They are used regularly (all the time).


is being eaten
are being eaten
has been eaten

Correct answer: are being eaten. Present Continuous: be being +V3. They are being eaten at this moment (right now).


is make
has being made
has been made

Correct answer: has been made. Present Perfect: has/have + been + V3. The result is visible.


is opened
was opened
has been opened

Correct answer: was opened. Past Simple: was/were + V3. An action which happened in the past (two weeks ago).


was said
was being said
was been said

Correct answer: was said. Past Simple: was/were +V3. An action in the past.


will be save
will be saved
will been saved

Correct answer: will be saved. Future Simple: will + be + V3.


is cooked
was cooked
has been cooked

Correct answer: was cooked. Past Simple: was/were + V3. Time marker: an hour ago.

Correct answer: will be written. Future Simple: will + be + V3. Time marker: soon.


was being crowded
is being crowded
will being crowded

Correct answer: was being crowded. Past Continuous: was/were + being + V3. An action was happening during some time in the past and was interrupted by a shorter action.

Correct answer: had been stolen. Past Perfect: had + been + V3. This action had happened before another action in the past.
